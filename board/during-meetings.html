During the Meeting

You scheduled a meeting, created, and then published the agenda. Now it’s time to do the real work. It’s time to get together, discuss, and take actions on the important issues. It’s time to have a board meeting.

During your board meeting you need someone to preside over the meeting and you need someone to take minutes.
