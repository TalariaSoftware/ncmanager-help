Governing Board

The governing board is probably the most well-known part of the neighborhood council system. In some senses, the board is the neighborhood council.

This guide is designed to give you everything you need to lead your board to success.

Start by understanding the role of the board in the neighborhood council system. Then take a look at what is required before, during, and after board meetings.
