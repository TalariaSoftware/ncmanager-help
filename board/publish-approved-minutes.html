Publish Approved Minutes

One of the most important things that an NC board does at a meeting is approve the minutes of the previous meeting. Approving the minutes shows that the board agrees that the minutes are a correct record of what transpired at the earlier meeting.

Once the minutes are approved they should be updated to show that they were approved and the date they were approved on. The updated minutes should be published on the NC website and wherever else your NC records are kept. Board of Neighborhood Commissioners policy requires this be done within thirty days.1

  1. “Approved written minutes of all board meetings shall be posted on the Neighborhood Council’s website … within 30 days of the regular or special meeting at which they are approved.” Los Angeles, CA, Board of Neighborhood Commissioners, Policy No. 2016-01 ↩
