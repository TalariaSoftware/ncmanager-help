Committees

Committees are the most important part of the neighborhood council system. They are where the work of the neighborhood councils gets done.

This guide is designed to give you everything you need to lead your committee to success.

Start by understanding the role of committees in the neighborhood council system. Then learn how a committee is established. Once your committee is established, take a look at what is required before, during, and after committee meetings.
