Approval Date

Minutes taken at a meeting become the official record of the assembly after they are approved by the assembly. Until then, they are draft minutes and should be clearly marked as such.1

NCMS makes it easy to create approved minutes.

For example:

Draft minutes — not yet approved

Once the minutes have been approved, they should state that, along with the date that they were approved.2

For example:

Approved on March 2, 2021
