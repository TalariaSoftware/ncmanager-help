Approval Date

Minutes taken at a meeting do not become the official record until they are approved. Until then they are draft minutes and should be clearly marked as such.1

For example:

Draft minutes --- not yet approved

Once the minutes have been approved, they should be marked as approved with the date they were approved on.2

For example:

Approved on March 2, 2021
