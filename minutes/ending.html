Meeting End

The body of the minutes should end with the last thing that happened in the meeting, to wit, the adjournment and the time it happened.1

Because it is the last thing that happened at the meeting, it should be the last item in the body of the meeting minutes.

For example:

[7:55 pm] Meeting adjourned.

  1. “The last paragraph should state: 12) the hour of adjournment.” RONR (12th ed.) 48:4 ↩
