permalink: /:path/:basename
---

The body
of the minutes
should end with
the last thing
that happened
in the meeting,
to wit,
the time of adjournment.[^ronradjourn]

For example:

    [7:55 pm] Meeting adjourned.

[^ronradjourn]:
    "The last paragraph
    should state:
    12) the hour
    of adjournment."
