Minutes

Minutes are the official record of neighborhood council meetings.1 They say what assembly met, when and where the meeting was held, what members were present, and what actions the assembly took.

This guide is designed to give you everything you need to create excellent minutes for your neighborhood council, minutes that are complete and compliant with all applicable rules and regulations.

  1. “The official record of the proceedings of a deliberative assembly is usually called the minutes.” RONR (12th ed.) 48:1 ↩
