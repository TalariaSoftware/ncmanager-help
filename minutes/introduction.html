Introduction

The first part of the minutes should indicate the name of the assembly, the date, time, and location of the meeting, and whether it is a regular or special meeting.1

Example of a regular board meeting introduction:

Lake Woebegone Neighborhood Council
Governing Board Regular Meeting
April 1, 2021 7:00 pm
Local Community Center
123 Lake St.
Los Angeles CA

Example of a special committee meeting introduction:

Lake Woebegone Neighborhood Council
Party Committee Special Meeting
April 1, 2021 7:00 pm
Local Community Center
123 Lake St.
Los Angeles CA

  1. RONR (12th ed.) 41:4(1)-(3) ↩
